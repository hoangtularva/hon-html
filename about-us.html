Company History

Mr. Moon Thai is a scientist. He researches natural organic minerals applied to improve and maintain a clean environment in various industries.

2010

Research stage of organic minerals.

2017

Product Research Stage.

2021

Established Heart of Nature Company.

13 March 2010

Research stage of organic minerals

In 2010, he has done many projects on environmental improvement in some rural areas around the world. After more than six years of research with many challenges, he found that the beneficial indicators for the soil environment, water environment, and plants after each research project gave better and more results.
17 March 2017
Product Research Stage
In 2017, he decided to acquire HON mine to continue researching natural organic minerals applied to improving the soil environment and water environment in many different fields.
2021

Established Heart of Nature Company

After time for researching the application of natural organic agricultural minerals with the expected results, in 2021, he decided to establish the Heart of Nature company in the US. The company operates in the manufacturing and trading of natural organic agricultural mineral products with the brand name S-K-Microsource.

Responsibility

100% of Nature

At Heart Of Nature, we do kindness for public health and act with integrity in everything we do. Our values guide us to make ethically sound decisions; responsible to consumers and society. The thoughtfulness in thought and action has driven all resources inside and outside the company towards unity, trust, and accountability to do the right thing. We all must understand our right to comply and carry out all duties with honor.

"We aim to protect the natural resources of land and water by making them cleaner. That purpose begins with farmers, ranchers, and businesses in the agricultural sector. We help our partners address the challenges of reclamation and restoration of land and water environments cleaner. A clean environment supports crops, and aquaculture grows better. That's how Heart of Nature and its partners make the world cleaner, healthier, and happier."
